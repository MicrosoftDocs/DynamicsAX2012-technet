# (RUS) Recalculate registers 


_**Applies To:** Microsoft Dynamics AX 2012 R3, Microsoft Dynamics AX 2012 R2_


> [!NOTE]
> <P>This topic has not been fully updated for Microsoft Dynamics AX 2012 R2.</P>



1.  Click **General ledger** \> **Journals** \> **Tax register journal**.

2.  Click **Reset status** to reset the status of all unapproved registers that are in the **Calculated** status.

3.  To reset the status of one unapproved register, select **Not calculated** in the **Status** field.

4.  Click **Register calculation** \> **Calculate current** to calculate the selected register.
    
    –or–
    
    Click **Calculate all** to calculate all journal registers.

5.  Click **OK** to confirm the calculation. After calculation, the status of the register is displayed as **Calculated** in the **Status** field.
    

    > [!NOTE]
    > <P>Click <STRONG>Register lines</STRONG> to view the updated register lines.</P>

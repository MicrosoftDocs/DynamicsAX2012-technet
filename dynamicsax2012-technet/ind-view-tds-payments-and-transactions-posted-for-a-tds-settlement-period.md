# (IND) View TDS payments and transactions posted for a TDS settlement period 


_**Applies To:** Microsoft Dynamics AX 2012 R3, Microsoft Dynamics AX 2012 R2_


> [!NOTE]
> <P>This topic has not been fully updated for Microsoft Dynamics AX 2012 R2.</P>



1.  Click **General ledger** \> **Setup** \> **Withholding tax** \> **India** \> **Withholding tax settlement periods**.
    

    > [!NOTE]
    > <P>For more information about withholding tax, see "Setting up withholding tax" in the Applications and Business Processes Help.</P>



2.  Click **Withholding tax payment** to open the **Withholding tax payment** form and view the TDS settlements made for a specific TDS settlement period.

3.  You can view the TDS settlement information such as the date range of the settlements and the withholding tax payment version on the **Overview** tab in the **Withholding**Â **tax payment** form.

4.  You can click the following buttons in the **Withholding tax payment** form.
    
      - **Withholding tax transactions** - Opens the **Withholding tax transactions** form, where you can view the details of TDS transactions that were settled for a specific period within a settlement period for the selected transaction in the Withholding tax payment form.
    
      - **Voucher** - Opens the **Voucher transactions** form, where you can view the voucher entries for the TDS transaction.

5.  In the **Withholding tax transactions** form, click **Withholding tax components** to view the posted TDS calculated per TDS tax component.

6.  Close the **Withholding tax components** form, the **Withholding tax transactions** form, and the **Withholding tax payment** form to return to the **Withholding tax settlement periods** form.

7.  Click **New period** to create a new settlement period in the **Periods** tab.

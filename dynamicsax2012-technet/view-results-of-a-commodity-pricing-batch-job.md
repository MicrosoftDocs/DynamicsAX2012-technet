# View results of a commodity pricing batch job 


_**Applies To:** Microsoft Dynamics AX 2012 R3, Microsoft Dynamics AX 2012 R2, Microsoft Dynamics AX 2012 Feature Pack, Microsoft Dynamics AX 2012_

Use this procedure to view the results of a commodity pricing batch job.

1.  Click **Inventory management** \> **Periodic** \> **Commodity pricing** \> **Price margin update**.

2.  In the **Pricing calculation** field, select the commodity pricing batch to view.

3.  Click the **Lines** button to display the **Price margin update-lines** form.

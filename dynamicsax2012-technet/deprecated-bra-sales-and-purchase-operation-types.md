# Deprecated: (BRA) Sales and purchase operation types 


_**Applies To:** Microsoft Dynamics AX 2012 R3, Microsoft Dynamics AX 2012 R2_

For Brazilian installations, Microsoft Dynamics AX 2009 provides country-specific modifications that support special transactions on sales and purchase orders through sales operation types and purchase operation types.

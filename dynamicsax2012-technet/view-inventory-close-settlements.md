# View inventory close settlements 


_**Applies To:** Microsoft Dynamics AX 2012 R3, Microsoft Dynamics AX 2012 R2, Microsoft Dynamics AX 2012 Feature Pack, Microsoft Dynamics AX 2012_

To view the items that are updated by running inventory close, use the **Settlements for voucher** form.

1.  Click **Inventory management** \> **Periodic** \> **Closing and adjustment**.

2.  In the **Closing and adjustment** form, on the **Overview** tab, select the record of the inventory closing to review.

3.  Click the **Settlements** button to open the **Settlements for voucher** form.

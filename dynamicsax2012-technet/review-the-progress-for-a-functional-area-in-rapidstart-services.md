# Review the progress for a functional area in RapidStart Services 


_**Applies To:** Microsoft Dynamics AX 2012 R3, Microsoft Dynamics AX 2012 R2, Microsoft Dynamics AX 2012 Feature Pack, Microsoft Dynamics AX 2012_

Beginning October 1, 2015, RapidStart services will no longer be supported. For more information, see the announcement on CustomerSourcehttps://mbs.microsoft.com/partnersource/northamerica/news-events/news/RapidStartServicesDiscontinued or PartnerSourcehttps://mbs.microsoft.com/customersource/northamerica/news-events/news-events/news/RapidStartServicesDiscontinued.

As the Customer administrator for a configuration project in Microsoft Dynamics ERP RapidStart Services, you can choose between two views of the progress that is being made on answering questions in the functional areas that are included in your configuration project. You can use either the **Projects** page or the **Functional area progress** page.

For each functional area, you can review the following information:

  - The percentage of questions that have been completed

  - The number of questions in the functional area

  - The number of questions that have been answered for the functional area

  - The number of questions that still have to be answered

  - The industry that is associated with the functional area

  - The country or region that is associated with the functional area (This information is available only on the **Projects** page.)

### Review the status of functional areas on the Projects page

1.  In your browser, open online services for Microsoft Dynamics ERP at [http://www.dynamicsonline.com](http://go.microsoft.com/fwlink/p/?linkid=141031).

2.  Click **For Customers**, and then click **Sign in**.

3.  Sign in by using the Microsoft account (formerly Windows Live ID) that is associated with your customer account in online services for Microsoft Dynamics ERP.

4.  If your logon credentials are associated with more than one organization in RapidStart Services, on the **Choose your organization** page, click the name of the organization to work with.

5.  On the Customer Portal dashboard, click **Microsoft Dynamics® RapidStart**.

6.  On the **Getting started** page, click **Open an existing project**.

7.  On the **Projects** page, click the row of the project for which to review the progress.

8.  In the lower half of the page, review the **Functional area progress** graph, and then click **Details** to open a table that contains details about the progress that is being made in the functional areas that are associated with the selected project.

### Review the status of functional areas on the Functional area progress page

1.  In your browser, open online services for Microsoft Dynamics ERP at [http://www.dynamicsonline.com](http://go.microsoft.com/fwlink/p/?linkid=141031).

2.  Click **For Customers**, and then click **Sign in**.

3.  Sign in by using the Microsoft account (formerly Windows Live ID) that is associated with your customer account in online services.

4.  If your logon credentials are associated with more than one organization in RapidStart Services, on the **Choose your organization** page, click the name of the organization to work with.

5.  On the Customer Portal dashboard, click **Microsoft Dynamics® RapidStart**.

6.  In the RapidStart Services header, click **Administer**, and then click **Reports**.

7.  In the list on the left side of the page, click **Functional area progress**.

8.  In the **Project** list, select the name of the configuration project for which to review the status of functional areas.
    
    –or–
    
    Scroll to the name of the configuration project for which to review the status of functional areas.

# (LTU) Set up United VAT invoice for customers 


_**Applies To:** Microsoft Dynamics AX 2012 R3, Microsoft Dynamics AX 2012 R2_

The sales documents issued by United VAT companies are marked to indicate that the VAT is paid by the United VAT company.


> [!NOTE]
> <P>This topic has not been fully updated for Microsoft Dynamics AX 2012 R2.</P>



1.  Click **Accounts receivable** \> **Common** \> **Customers** \> **All customers**.

2.  Double-click a customer from the list to open the **Customers** form.

3.  Click the **Invoice and delivery** FastTab.

4.  Select the **United VAT invoice** check box to indicate that the VAT is paid by the United VAT company for the customer.

5.  Press CTRL+S or close the form.

# (IND) View sales tax inquiries 


_**Applies To:** Microsoft Dynamics AX 2012 R3, Microsoft Dynamics AX 2012 R2_


> [!NOTE]
> <P>This topic has not been fully updated for Microsoft Dynamics AX 2012 R2.</P>



You can inquire and generate reports to view details of transactions with multiple taxes. According to the type of tax selected, you can view the following information:

  - The total tax amount for a specific period.

  - The vouchers that are attached to the transactions with multiple taxations.

  - The tax amounts for the tax components of the other tax types.

  - The miscellaneous charges that are attached to the transactions.

These amounts are displayed in the default currency and the secondary currency of the company for the sales tax transactions.

## Indirect tax inquiry for multiple taxes

You can specify the criteria to list tax transactions that are posted for multiple taxes, such as Value Added Tax (VAT), service tax, excise, customs duty, and sales tax in the **Indirect tax inquiry** form. You can also list the transactions for a specific tax type for a specific period.


> [!NOTE]
> <P>You must select the appropriate check boxes in the <STRONG>General ledger parameters</STRONG> form to activate the taxes.</P>

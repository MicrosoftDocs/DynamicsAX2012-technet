# (IND) View transactions with sales tax 


[!INCLUDE[archive-banner](includes/archive-banner.md)]


_**Applies To:** Microsoft Dynamics AX 2012 R3, Microsoft Dynamics AX 2012 R2_


> [!NOTE]
> <P>This topic has not been fully updated for Microsoft Dynamics AX 2012 R2.</P>



You can use the **India sales tax inquiry** form to view all transactions for the selected India sales tax registration number and the tax settlement period. This form is available only when the check boxes in the **Apply India taxes** group are selected on the **Sales tax** tab in the **General ledger parameters** form.

1.  Click **General ledger** \> **Inquiries** \> **Tax** \> **India posted tax** \> **India sales tax** \> **India sales tax**.

2.  Select the query to modify in the **Select query** field.

3.  Click **Modify** to modify, save, or delete the query.

4.  Select the registration number for the company's address in the **Registration number** field.

5.  Enter the tax settlement period for the selected registration number in the **Tax settlement period** field.

6.  Select the starting date and ending dates in the **From date** and **To date** fields.

7.  Select the required fields from the **Available** list.

8.  Click **\>** to add items from the **Available** to the **Selected** list.

9.  Click the **\<** to remove items from the **Selected** list.

10. Click **Top**, **Up**, **Down**, or **Bottom** to rearrange the order of items.

11. Select or clear the **Header note** check box to indicate whether the header note must be defined and displayed, and then enter the text for the header note in the **Header note** field, if needed.

12. Select or clear the **Footer note** check box to indicate whether the footer note must be defined and displayed, and then enter the text for the footer note in the **Footer note** field, if needed.

13. Click **Inquiry** to create or update the selected query in the **Inquiry** form.
    

    > [!NOTE]
    > <P>For more information, see "Inquiry (form)" in the Applications and Business Processes Help.</P>



14. Click **Total** to select the amount fields that are to be summed up. Only the amount-related fields that are in the Selected list are added in this form. Close the **Total** form.

15. Click **OK** to open the **India sales tax transactions** form, where you can view the details of the inquiry.

16. You can click the following buttons in the **India sales tax transactions** form to view additional information.
    
      - **Voucher** - Opens the **Voucher transactions** form, where you can view ledger account details.
    
      - **Totals** - Opens the **Totals - India sales tax** form, where you can view the total amount.
    
      - **Tax components** - Opens the **Tax components - India sales tax** form, where you can view sales tax information for the tax components and currency details.
    
      - **Charges** - Opens the **Misc. charges transactions** form, where you can view the details of the miscellaneous charges transactions.

17. Close the **India sales tax transactions** form.

## Remarks

Of the form expression: +\[BCD\]+\[CVD\]+\[E-CESS\_CVD\]+\[PE-C\_CVD\]+\[SHE-C\_CVD\] where the brackets are replaced with the delimiter char(164) and BCD, CVD ... are tax codes.

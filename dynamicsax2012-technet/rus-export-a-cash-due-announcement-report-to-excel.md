# (RUS) Export a cash due announcement report to Excel [AX 2012]


_**Applies To:** Microsoft Dynamics AX 2012 R3, Microsoft Dynamics AX 2012 R2_


> [!NOTE]
> <P>This topic has not been fully updated for Microsoft Dynamics AX 2012 R2.</P>



1.  Click **Cash and bank management** \> **Inquiries** \> **Cash due announcement**.

2.  Select the required journal.

3.  Click **Print** to print the cash due announcement report (unified report 0402001.xlt) for the registered bank when updating the journal.

4.  Press CTRL+S or close the form.

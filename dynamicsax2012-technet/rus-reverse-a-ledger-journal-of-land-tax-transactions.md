# (RUS) Reverse a ledger journal of land tax transactions 


_**Applies To:** Microsoft Dynamics AX 2012 R3, Microsoft Dynamics AX 2012 R2_

Use this procedure to reverse a ledger journal of land tax transactions.

1.  Click **Fixed assets (Russia)** \> **Journals** \> **Tax register journal**. Select a tax register journal, and then click **Ledger journal** \> **Land tax**.

2.  Select a ledger journal, and then click **Create** \> **Reverse journal** to cancel the journal that was most recently created. Journal lines for canceled entries are added for the land tax journal that was most recently posted. The journal is posted automatically.
    

    > [!NOTE]
    > <P>The <STRONG>Reversed</STRONG> field in the <STRONG>Tax register journal</STRONG> form is updated after the most recently created journal is reversed.</P>



3.  Click **Lines** to view the posted transactions.

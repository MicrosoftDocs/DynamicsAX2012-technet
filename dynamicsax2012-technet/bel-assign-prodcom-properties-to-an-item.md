# (BEL) Assign PRODCOM properties to an item 


[!INCLUDE[archive-banner](includes/archive-banner.md)]


_**Applies To:** Microsoft Dynamics AX 2012 R3, Microsoft Dynamics AX 2012 R2, Microsoft Dynamics AX 2012 Feature Pack, Microsoft Dynamics AX 2012_

Use the **PRODCOM** form to assign PRODCOM properties to an item.

1.  Click **Product information management** \> **Common** \> **Released products**.

2.  In the **Released products** list page, double-click an item line to open the **Released product details** form.

3.  Click the **Foreign trade** FastTab, and then click **PRODCOM** to open the **PRODCOM** dialog.

4.  Select the appropriate check boxes – **Product made in company**, **Delivery to a third party**, or **Work done for enterprises** – to report the quantities and values of products that were delivered by a company, a third party, or an enterprise.

5.  Click **OK** to return to the **Released product details** form.

6.  Close the forms to save your changes.

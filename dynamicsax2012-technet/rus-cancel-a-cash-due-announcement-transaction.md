# (RUS) Cancel a cash due announcement transaction 


_**Applies To:** Microsoft Dynamics AX 2012 R3, Microsoft Dynamics AX 2012 R2_


> [!NOTE]
> <P>This topic has not been fully updated for Microsoft Dynamics AX 2012 R2.</P>



1.  Click **General ledger** \> **Journals** \> **General journal**. Click **Lines** to open the **Journal voucher** form.
    
    –or–
    
    Click **Cash and bank management** \> **Journals** \> **Slip journal**. Click **Lines** to open the **Journal voucher** form.

2.  Select the journal that contains the cash due announcement to be canceled.

3.  Click **Functions** \> **Cancel** **Cash due announcement.**

4.  Click **Cancel cash due announcement** in the **Cash due announcement journal** form.

5.  Click **OK** to confirm cancelation.
    

    > [!NOTE]
    > <P>The <STRONG>Cash due announcement</STRONG>, <STRONG>Document</STRONG>, and <STRONG>Document date</STRONG> fields are blank and editable after the cash due announcement is canceled.</P>



6.  Press CTRL+S or close the form.

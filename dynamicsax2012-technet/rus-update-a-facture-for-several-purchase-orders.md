# (RUS) Update a facture for several purchase orders 


_**Applies To:** Microsoft Dynamics AX 2012 R3, Microsoft Dynamics AX 2012 R2_


> [!NOTE]
> <P>This topic has not been fully updated for Microsoft Dynamics AX 2012 R2.</P>



In general, only one invoice is included in each facture. However, sometimes it is necessary to include more than one invoice in a facture.

1.  Click **Accounts payable** \> **Journals** \> **Invoices** \> **Invoice journal**.

2.  Select the invoice account, and then click **Update facture** to open the **Update facture** form.

3.  In the **Facture** field, enter the facture number.

4.  In the **Date of the registration** field, select the facture registration date.

5.  In the **Facture date** field, select the facture creation date.
    

    > [!NOTE]
    > <P>This date is the same as the facture registration date. The field can be modified only if you clear the <STRONG>Same as</STRONG> check box.</P>



6.  In the lower section of the form, on the **Invoice** tab, select the **To facture** check box to include the invoices for the facture.
    

    > [!NOTE]
    > <P>If you do not want to create a facture for a particular invoice line, click the <STRONG>Invoice lines</STRONG> tab and clear the <STRONG>To facture</STRONG> check box.</P>



7.  Click **Posting** \> **Update and print** to update and print the facture.
    
    –or–
    
    Click **Posting** \> **Update** to update the facture without printing.
    
    –or–
    
    Click **Posting** \> **Print** to print the facture without posting.
    

    > [!NOTE]
    > <P>Click <STRONG>Accounts payable</STRONG> &gt; <STRONG>Inquiries</STRONG> &gt; <STRONG>Journals</STRONG> &gt; <STRONG>Facture</STRONG>.</P>

# (BRA) Set up post interest and fines with centralized payment for customers 


_**Applies To:** Microsoft Dynamics AX 2012 R3, Microsoft Dynamics AX 2012 R2_


> [!NOTE]
> <P>This topic has not been fully updated for Microsoft Dynamics AX 2012 R2.</P>



1.  Click **General ledger** \> **Setup** \> **Posting** \> **Intercompany accounting**.

2.  Select an intercompany account for a company that you will process payments on behalf of.

3.  Click the **Centralized payments** tab.

4.  In the **Post interest** field in the **Centralized customer payments** field group, select whether to post the interest amount to the legal entity receiving the payment or to the legal entity issuing the invoice.

5.  In the **Post fine** field, in the **Centralized customer payments** field group, select whether to post fine amount to the legal entity receiving the payment or to the legal entity issuing the invoice.

6.  Press CTRL+S or close the form.

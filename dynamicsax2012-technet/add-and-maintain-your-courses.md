# Add and maintain your courses 


_**Applies To:** Microsoft Dynamics AX 2012 R3, Microsoft Dynamics AX 2012 R2, Microsoft Dynamics AX 2012 Feature Pack, Microsoft Dynamics AX 2012_

Use the **Competencies** page to add or maintain information about courses that you have completed or are registered for. You can add internal courses (courses set up in Microsoft Dynamics AX) or external courses.


> [!NOTE]
> <P>Depending on your role or the privileges that are assigned to you, you might need to go to your Employee services site before you complete the procedures in this topic.</P>



## View a course

1.  Click **Personal information** on the top link bar, and then click **Competencies** on the Quick Launch.

2.  On the **Courses** FastTab, click a course link to view information about the course.

## Add a course

1.  Click **Personal information** on the top link bar, and then click **Competencies** on the Quick Launch.

2.  On the **Courses** FastTab, click **Add** to display the **New course** page.

3.  If the course was set up in Microsoft Dynamics AX, select the course ID associated with the course and go to step 7. If the course was not set up in Microsoft Dynamics AX, go to the next step.

4.  Enter the location of the course.

5.  Enter the name of the course.

6.  Enter the start and end dates for the course.

7.  Click **Save and close**.

## Modify a course

1.  Click **Personal information** on the top link bar, and then click **Competencies** on the Quick Launch.

2.  On the **Courses** FastTab, select the course to modify.

3.  Click **Edit**.

4.  Modify or enter information in the necessary fields and then click **Save and close**.

## Delete a course

1.  Click **Personal information** on the top link bar, and then click **Competencies** on the Quick Launch.

2.  On the **Courses** FastTab, select the course to delete and then click **Remove**.

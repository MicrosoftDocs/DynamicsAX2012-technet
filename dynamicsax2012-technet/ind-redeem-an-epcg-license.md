# (IND) Redeem an EPCG license 


[!INCLUDE[archive-banner](includes/archive-banner.md)]


_**Applies To:** Microsoft Dynamics AX 2012 R3, Microsoft Dynamics AX 2012 R2_

You can redeem an Export Promotion Capital Goods (EPCG) license if the status of the license is **Approved**. After a license is redeemed, the status of the license cannot be changed.

1.  Click **General ledger** \> **Common** \> **EXIM EPCG Schemes**.

2.  In the **EXIM EPCG Schemes** list, select an EPCG scheme that has a status of **Approved**.

3.  In the **EXIM EPCG Schemes** form, click **Functions**, and then click **Redeemed**.

4.  Click **Yes** to confirm redemption of the selected EPCG license.

In the **EXIM EPCG Schemes** list, the status of the EPCG license is **Redeemed**.

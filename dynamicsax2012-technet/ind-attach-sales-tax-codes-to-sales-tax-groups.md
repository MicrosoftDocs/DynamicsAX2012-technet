# (IND) Attach sales tax codes to sales tax groups 


_**Applies To:** Microsoft Dynamics AX 2012 R3, Microsoft Dynamics AX 2012 R2_


> [!NOTE]
> <P>This topic has not been fully updated for Microsoft Dynamics AX 2012 R2.</P>



You can create sales tax groups and attach sales tax codes with LST (Local Sales Tax) form types, CST (Central Sales Tax) form types, or both the form types to the groups.

1.  Click **General ledger** \> **Setup** \> **Sales tax** \> **Sales tax groups**.
    

    > [!NOTE]
    > <P>For more information, see <A href="https://technet.microsoft.com/en-us/library/aa498345(v=ax.60)">Sales tax groups (form)</A>.</P>



2.  Enter the required details to attach the sales tax code to the sales tax group.

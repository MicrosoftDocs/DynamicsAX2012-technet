# (IND) Generate bills of entry inquiries for import orders [AX 2012]


_**Applies To:** Microsoft Dynamics AX 2012 R3, Microsoft Dynamics AX 2012 R2_

Generate a bill of entry inquiry to view the posted bill of entry transaction details for import orders.


> [!NOTE]
> <P>This topic has not been fully updated for Microsoft Dynamics AX 2012 R2.</P>



1.  Click **Accounts payable** \> **Inquiries** \> **Journals** \> **Bill of entry**.

2.  Click the **Overview** FastTab to view the bill of entry details, and click the **Lines** tab to view the posted bill of entry transactions.

3.  Click the **Lines** FastTab, and then click **Inventory** \> **Dimension display** to activate the fields to be included in the **Bill of entry journal** form.

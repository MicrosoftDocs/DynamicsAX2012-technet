# (IND) View TCS payments and transactions posted for a TCS settlement period 


_**Applies To:** Microsoft Dynamics AX 2012 R3, Microsoft Dynamics AX 2012 R2_


> [!NOTE]
> <P>This topic has not been fully updated for Microsoft Dynamics AX 2012 R2.</P>



1.  Click **General ledger** \> **Setup** \> **Withholding tax** \> **India** \> **Withholding tax settlement periods**.

2.  Click **Withholding tax payment** to view the TCS settlement made for a specific TCS settlement period. The **Withholding tax payment** form opens.

3.  Click **Voucher** to open the **Voucher transactions** form, where you can view the voucher entries for the TCS transaction.

4.  Click **Withholding tax transactions** to open the **Withholding tax transactions** form, where you can view the details of TCS transactions that were settled for a specific period in a settlement period.

You can click **Voucher** to view the voucher entries for the TCS transaction or the **Tax components** button to view the TCS that is calculated per TCS tax component for a specific TCS tax code.

# Resume inventory recalculation 


_**Applies To:** Microsoft Dynamics AX 2012 R3, Microsoft Dynamics AX 2012 R2, Microsoft Dynamics AX 2012 Feature Pack, Microsoft Dynamics AX 2012_

If inventory recalculation has been paused, it cannot be completed until you resume the process.

1.  Click **Inventory management** \> **Periodic** \> **Closing and adjustment**.

2.  In the **Closing and adjustment** form, on the **Overview** tab, select the record that you want to review from the inventory closing.

3.  Click **Calculation** \> **Resume calculation**.

# View the status of open sales orders 


_**Applies To:** Microsoft Dynamics AX 2012 R3, Microsoft Dynamics AX 2012 R2, Microsoft Dynamics AX 2012 Feature Pack, Microsoft Dynamics AX 2012_

The following steps describe how to view the status of open sales orders.

1.  Click **Sales and marketing** \> **Inquiries** \> **Order status** \> **Open sales order lines**.

2.  On the **Overview** tab, select the order to check the status for.
    
    You can also view information such as the ship date and the remaining quantity that has to be delivered.

3.  Click the **Sales order** tab to view customer information and the status of the order.

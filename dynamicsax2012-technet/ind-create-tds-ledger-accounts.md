# (IND) Create TDS ledger accounts 


_**Applies To:** Microsoft Dynamics AX 2012 R3, Microsoft Dynamics AX 2012 R2_


> [!NOTE]
> <P>This topic has not been fully updated for Microsoft Dynamics AX 2012 R2.</P>



You can define receivable accounts to post the TDS receivable amount for TDS tax codes. These accounts must use **Withholding tax** as the posting type.

1.  Click **General ledger** \> **Setup** \> **Chart of accounts** \> **Chart of accounts**. On the **Main accounts** FastTab, double-click a main account, or click **New**.

2.  On the **Overview** tab, press CTRL+N to create a new main account and enter the required details.

3.  Click the **Setup** tab.

4.  Select **Withholding tax** in the **Posting type** field.

5.  Press CTRL+S or close the form.

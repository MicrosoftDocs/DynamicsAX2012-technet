# (IND) Transfer of capital items to the fixed assets 


_**Applies To:** Microsoft Dynamics AX 2012 R3, Microsoft Dynamics AX 2012 R2_


> [!NOTE]
> <P>This topic has not been fully updated for Microsoft Dynamics AX 2012 R2.</P>



You should transfer a capital item by issuing the inventory items to the Fixed assets journal.

1.  Click **Fixed assets** \> **Journals** \> **Inventory to fixed assets**.

2.  Select a journal and click the **Lines** button.

3.  Select the fixed asset from the **Fixed asset number** field.
    

    > [!NOTE]
    > <P>You can select the <STRONG>Acquisition adjustment</STRONG> in the <STRONG>Transaction type</STRONG> field.</P>



4.  Select the capital item in the **Item number** field that was created in the **Items** form, and enter the quantity of the capital item in the **Quantity** field.

5.  Validate and post the journal.

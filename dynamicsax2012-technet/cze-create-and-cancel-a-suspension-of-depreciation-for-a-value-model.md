# (CZE) Create and cancel a suspension of depreciation for a value model 


_**Applies To:** Microsoft Dynamics AX 2012 R3, Microsoft Dynamics AX 2012 R2_


> [!NOTE]
> <P>This topic has not been fully updated for Microsoft Dynamics AX 2012 R2.</P>



1.  Click **Fixed assets** \> **Common** \> **Fixed assets** \> **Fixed assets**.

2.  In the **Fixed asset group** field, select a fixed asset group and click **Value models** to open the **Value models** form.

3.  In the **Value model** field, select the value model to be used.
    

    > [!NOTE]
    > <P>You must create a value model in the <STRONG>Fixed asset group/value model</STRONG> form with the posting layer set as <STRONG>Tax</STRONG>.</P>



4.  Click **Suspension** to open the **Depreciation suspension** form.

5.  Click **Create new suspension** to open the **Create depreciation suspension** form.

6.  In the **Suspension period** field, enter the year of suspension and click **OK**.
    

    > [!NOTE]
    > <P>Select a year that is later than the year that the fixed asset was acquired.</P>



7.  Click **Cancel depreciation suspension** to open the **Cancel depreciation suspension** form and cancel the depreciation suspension.

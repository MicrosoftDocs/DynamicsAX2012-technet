# Deprecated: (BRA) Complementary fiscal documents from free text invoices and purchase orders 


_**Applies To:** Microsoft Dynamics AX 2012 R3, Microsoft Dynamics AX 2012 R2_

For Brazilian installations, Microsoft Dynamics AX 2009 provides country-specific modifications that support complementary fiscal documents. These documents are issued from free text invoices for sales and from purchase orders for purchases.

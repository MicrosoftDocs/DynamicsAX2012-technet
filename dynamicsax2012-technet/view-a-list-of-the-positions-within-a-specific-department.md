# View a list of the positions within a specific department [AX 2012]


_**Applies To:** Microsoft Dynamics AX 2012 R3, Microsoft Dynamics AX 2012 R2, Microsoft Dynamics AX 2012 Feature Pack, Microsoft Dynamics AX 2012_

To view a list of positions within a specific department, complete one of the following procedures.

## Filter the Positions list page by department

1.  Click **Human resources** \> **Common** \> **Organization** \> **Positions** \> **Positions**.

2.  In the filter pane, select the **Department** column from the list.

3.  Type the name of the department to display positions for and press ENTER.

## Print the Positions by department report

Click **Human resources** \> **Reports** \> **Organization** \> **Positions by department**. Modify the report parameters, as necessary, and then click **OK**.

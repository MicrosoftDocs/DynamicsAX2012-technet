# (RUS) Post taxes in a general journal for vendors 


_**Applies To:** Microsoft Dynamics AX 2012 R3, Microsoft Dynamics AX 2012 R2_


> [!NOTE]
> <P>This topic has not been fully updated for Microsoft Dynamics AX 2012 R2.</P>



1.  Click **General ledger** \> **Journals** \> **General journal**.

2.  Select a journal and then press CTRL +N to create a new line, and enter the required details.

3.  In the **Date** field, select the voucher posting date.

4.  In the **Account type** field, select **Vendor**.

5.  In the **Account** field, select the vendor account number.

6.  In the **Credit** field, enter the credit amount, including taxes.

7.  Click the **General** tab. In the **Sales tax group** field, select the sales tax group to determine the tax.

8.  In the **Item sales tax group** field, select the item sales tax group to determine the tax.

9.  Click the **Invoice** tab. In the **Posting profile** field, select a posting profile code to assign the vendor debt to the correct ledger account.

10. In the **Invoice** field, enter the invoice number.

11. Click **Sales tax** to view the calculated sales tax for each line.

12. Click **Post** \> **Post** to post the journal.
    

    > [!NOTE]
    > <P>To view the ledger calculations created for the journal, in the <STRONG>Journal voucher</STRONG> form, click <STRONG>Inquiries</STRONG> &gt; <STRONG>Voucher</STRONG>.</P>

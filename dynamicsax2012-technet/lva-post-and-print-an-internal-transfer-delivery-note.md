# (LVA) Post and print an internal transfer delivery note 


_**Applies To:** Microsoft Dynamics AX 2012 R3, Microsoft Dynamics AX 2012 R2_

Use the **Transfer delivery note** form to print a transfer delivery note.


> [!NOTE]
> <P>This topic has not been fully updated for Microsoft Dynamics AX 2012 R2.</P>



1.  Click **Inventory management** \> **Journals** \> **Item transactions** \> **Transfer**.

2.  Press CTRL+N to create a new line.

3.  In the **Name** field, select the journal name.

4.  Click **Lines** to open the **Journal lines, Inventory** form.

5.  In the **Item number** field, select the item number of the inventory item that is to be posted.

6.  In the **From warehouse** field, select the warehouse that the goods are moving from.

7.  In the **To warehouse** field, select the warehouse that the goods are moving to.

8.  Click **Post** to open the **Post journal** dialog box.

9.  Click **OK** to post the journal.

10. Close the **Journal lines, Inventory** form.

11. In the **Transfer** form, click **Print** and then select **Transfer delivery note**.

12. In the **Transfer delivery note** form, select the code of the commission employee who approves the inventory journal in the **Commission employee names** field group.
    

    > [!NOTE]
    > <P>You can select up to four commission employee codes, if needed.</P>



13. In the **Reason of document** field, enter the reason for the movement of goods.

14. Click **OK** to print the report.

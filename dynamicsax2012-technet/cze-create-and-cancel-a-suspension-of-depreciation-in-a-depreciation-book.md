# (CZE) Create and cancel a suspension of depreciation in a depreciation book 


_**Applies To:** Microsoft Dynamics AX 2012 R3, Microsoft Dynamics AX 2012 R2_


> [!NOTE]
> <P>This topic has not been fully updated for Microsoft Dynamics AX 2012 R2.</P>



1.  Click **Fixed assets** \> **Common** \> **Fixed assets** \> **Fixed assets**. Select a fixed asset. On the **Action Pane**, click **Depreciation books**.

2.  In the **Depreciation book** field, select the depreciation book to be used.

3.  Click **Suspension** to open the **Depreciation suspension** form.

4.  Click **Create new suspension** to open the **Create depreciation suspension** form.

5.  In the **Suspension period** field, enter the year of suspension and click **OK**.
    

    > [!NOTE]
    > <P>Select a year that is later than the year that the fixed asset was acquired.</P>



6.  Click **Cancel depreciation suspension** to open the **Cancel depreciation suspension** form.

7.  Click **OK** to cancel the depreciation suspension.

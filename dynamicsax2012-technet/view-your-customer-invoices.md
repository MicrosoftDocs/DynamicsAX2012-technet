# View your customer invoices 


_**Applies To:** Microsoft Dynamics AX 2012 R3, Microsoft Dynamics AX 2012 R2, Microsoft Dynamics AX 2012 Feature Pack, Microsoft Dynamics AX 2012_

You can use the **Customer invoice** page to view a list of your customer invoices and to view the details of each invoice. You also can view copies of invoices that have been sent.

For information about returning an item that you have ordered, see [Return an item](return-an-item.md).

1.  Click **Customer self-service** on the top link bar, and then click **Invoices** on the Quick Launch.

2.  To view the details of an invoice, select the invoice in the list. On the **Action Pane**, on the **Customer invoice** tab, in the **Maintain** group, click **View invoice**.
